Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center

BIDMC is one of our largest clients. We have a standing relationship and are currently working on several solutions for their Emergency Medicine department. Our projects include:

Mobile Research Applications

We developed an application for research helping patients complete breathing exercises to better recover from surgery.

Operations Dashboards and Visualiziation

We're working with the Emergency Medicine Operations team to help them get a better picture of the department both in real time and historically.

Data Science Research

When is the Emergency Department most dangerous to patients? We're part of a team developing a machine learning model to identify falls and other hazards before they occur.

Clinical Web Applications

We're enabling physicians to create and print medical procedure consent forms and more.
